Magnapinna Squids

(Demo site for GCI 2019/2020)

The agnapinna squid, aka the bigfin squid, is a deep-sea species of squid found that has been sighted off of the coast of Brazil, the Gulf of Mexico, and near Hawaii. This species of squid has a very peculiar form, with arms that make up 90% of its body. Little is known about the squid's feeding behavior or biology because a specimine has never been captured for studying.

Sightings of Magnapinna Squid

  • North Coast of Brazil - September 1988
  • Coast of Ghana - July 1992
  • Indian Ocean - November 1998
  • Gulf of Mexico - January 2000
  • Rodrigues Island - May 2000
  • North of Hawaii - May 2001
  • Off of Peridido - November 2007
